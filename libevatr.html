libevatr

libevatr is a Java library which provides access to the online XML-RPC service of federal central office for taxes (in Germany) to confirm value added tax identification numbers and addresses of foreign (European) companies.

Features

* simple validation: confirms the foreign VAT ID number only

* qualified validation: confirms the foreign VAT ID number, company name and city
Within the qualified validation optionally the ZIP code and street can be confirmed. Additional a printed confirmation for your request can be requested. It will be send out by the federal central office for taxes by postal mail.
note: The confirmation will be send to the address linked to your company VAT ID.

The official page on the website of federal central office for taxes:
http://evatr.bff-online.de/eVatR/xmlrpc
